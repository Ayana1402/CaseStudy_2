Alappuzha Beach, Kerala's coastal gem, captivates with its golden sands, serene waters, and historic pier. A haven for sunset admirers, it offers a relaxed ambience for leisurely strolls, beach activities, and cultural events. The beach's beauty, highlighted by its verdant surroundings, makes it a must-visit destination in the region.

Alappuzha's backwaters weave an enchanting tapestry of serene lakes, meandering canals, and lush landscapes. Embarking on a houseboat or canoe journey through this intricate waterway network unveils a tranquil world where rural life unfolds, offering an authentic glimpse into the heart of Kerala's captivating culture and natural beauty.

The Ambalapuzha Temple is one of Alappuzha's most important temples. This temple, dedicated to Lord Krishna, is a must-see.The idol at Ambalappuzha is likened to Parthasarthi form of Vishnu, holding a whip in his right hand and a conch in his left.
